About Us

At Agronova, we believe in the power of technology to transform agriculture and improve the lives of farmers. Our mission is to create innovative solutions that help farmers make smarter decisions and boost crop yields sustainably.
With the Fertilizer Optimizer, we've taken a step toward empowering farmers by simplifying the science behind soil nutrition. By using cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, our tool predicts the ideal NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) fertilizer quantities based on soil and crop data, ensuring that each field gets exactly what it needs.
